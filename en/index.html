Mining License Types

We extract all kinds of licenses available via the electronic mining platform

01

Exploration License

Gives the investor the exclusive right to explore for specific minerals in a specific area through surface mining and geophysical/geochemical analysis.

02

Survey License

Gives the investor the right to explore for target minerals by visiting selected areas in addition to collecting samples from specified lands.

03

Mining License

Gives the investor the right to mine and exploit specified minerals from categories (A) and (B) in addition to transporting and selling exploited minerals with operational rights.

04

Small Mine License

Gives the investor the exclusive right to extract ores and minerals (mining and quarrying) through a mine whose area does not exceed one square kilometer.

05

Building Materials License

Gives the investor the right to mine and exploit specified minerals from category (C) in addition to transporting and selling exploited minerals with full operational rights.

06

Surplus Ore License

Allows for the transportation of category (C) mineral ores off-site for the purpose of selling them or benefiting from them, located in project sites or owned lands.

07

General Purpose License

Allows an investor holding a mining license or small mine license to establish facilities or use lands outside the license site to achieve license purposes.
